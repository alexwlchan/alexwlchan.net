This is the sequel to [*Punderworld*][me-punderworld], in which Persephone has come to visit Hades in the Underworld but is now unable to leave.
The characters are cute and the art style is gorgeous; it's exactly what I'd expect from this book.

I'm also intrigued by what's going to happen next -- the usual story about a pomegranate is dismissed, so whatever's up is going to diverge from the narrative I know.

Bring on volume 3!
