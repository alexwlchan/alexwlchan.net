A nice collection of poems about being queer, Black history, and Greek Cypriot heritage.
As with most poetry collections, I deliberately didn't read the summary first, because I wanted to find the story through the poems.
It came through clearly -- their stories of moving, living apart from family during COVID, their heritage and history.

I saved a couple of the poems, and I'd happily read another anthology by this author.
