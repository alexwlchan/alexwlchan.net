Flickr ([www.flickr.com](https://www.flickr.com/)) is a social media site for sharing photos that launched in 2004, and still exists today.

I worked at the [Flickr Foundation](/work/flickr-foundation/) between 2023 and 2025, where we explored ways to keep Flickr pictures visible for 100 years.
These entries are my notes on working with Flickr and Flickr's API.

If you're interested in this, you may also want to read about [preserving social media](/digital-preservation/social-media/).
