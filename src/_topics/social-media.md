title: Preserving social media
---

Social media is modern culture, but it's very difficult to preserve.
Social media platforms strongly resist being archived or preserved.

I spent two years working on this problem while at [the Flickr Foundation](https://www.flickr.org/), where I focused on preserving a single social media network.

This topic has my notes on preserving social media, including my current personal practice.
